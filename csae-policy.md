Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE) Policy
Effective Date: June 27, 2026

1. Zero Tolerance Policy
SA ChatApp has a strict, zero-tolerance policy against any form of Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). We are committed to providing a safe environment for all users and strongly condemn the exploitation or abuse of children.

Any user found to be promoting, sharing, or engaging in activities related to CSAE will face immediate and permanent suspension from our platform.

2. Prohibited Content and Behavior
Users of SA ChatApp are strictly prohibited from uploading, sharing, or promoting any content that involves:

The sexual abuse, exploitation, or sexualization of minors (anyone under the age of 18).
Solicitation of minors for sexual purposes.
Distribution or promotion of child sexual abuse material (CSAM).
Grooming or any predatory behavior directed towards minors.
3. Reporting and Enforcement
If you encounter any content or behavior on SA ChatApp that you suspect violates our CSAE policy, we strongly urge you to report it immediately.

Our Action Plan:
Upon receiving a report of potential CSAE violations, SA ChatApp will take the following actions:

Immediate Removal: The offending content will be removed immediately.
Account Termination: The account(s) responsible for the violation will be permanently disabled and banned from the platform.
Law Enforcement Cooperation: We comply with all applicable laws and will report incidents involving CSAM to the appropriate authorities, such as the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) or relevant local law enforcement agencies.
4. User Age Verification
SA ChatApp is strictly for users who are 18 years of age or older. We implement measures to prevent minors from accessing the app. If we discover that a user is under the age of 18, their account will be terminated immediately.
